About Us

Hung Vo Thanh

I am Hung Vo Thanh. A research geoscientist with more than 10 years of industry, academia, and research experience in reservoir engineering, petroleum geoscience and geological carbon utilization and storage.

Specialist in reservoir simulation, 3D geological modelling, machine learning, deep neural networks, convolutional neural network. Special experience in reservoir simulation and machine learning for Geological Carbon Utilization Storage and reservoir engineering.

PhD in Earth Resources Engineering with distinction and published 37 international research papers, including 27 in peer-reviewed SCI journals of ‘Q1’ and ‘Q2’. Visited in the UK, Indonesia, Philippines, Japan and South Korea for presenting research papers in SPE conference, EAGE conference, and JFES workshops. A potential reviewer of Springer Nature, Elsevier, Wiley, and SPE journals. An Editor of Journal of Petroleum Exploration and Production Technology, Springer Nature (SCIE, Q2).
